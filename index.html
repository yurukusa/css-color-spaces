Why oklch > hsl?

• Perceptually uniform: L=50% looks equally bright at any hue
• Wide gamut: Access P3/Rec2020 colors
• Predictable chroma: Same C value = same perceived saturation
• Better gradients: No gray dead zones
• Palette friendly: Rotate hue, keep L/C for harmony
